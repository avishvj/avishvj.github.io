About me

I’m currently doing my PhD in AI for Healthcare at Imperial with a focus on clinical multi-omics. On the ML side, I’m working on interpretability, multi-modal learning, graph and topological ML, and NLP, and on the medical side, IPF, Parkinson’s, and histopathology workflows. Before the PhD, I did a bachelor’s in maths and computer science at Imperial and a master’s in precision medicine at UCL. My CV is available here.

On the careers side, I’ve done a couple research internships - one with Microsoft Research’s BioML team, and another between the NHS and Great Ormond Street Hospital’s Data Science teams. I’ve stayed on part time at Great Ormond Street Hospital and I’m a fellow at No Patient Left Behind, an organisation dedicated to making pharma innovation affordable via health insurance reform in the US. I’ve also tutored GCSE, A-Level, and undergraduate students, and done life sciences consulting work.

If you’d like to get in contact, drop me a message on LinkedIn, X, or email. Outside of research, I occasionally write, make videos, and make tunes.
